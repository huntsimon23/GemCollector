Collect the Infinity Gems!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 Infinity Gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given to you, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Here's the trick: The value of ech crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of hte crystals.

Good luck! Stop Thanos before he snaps...
